OpenMandriva mirrors

Distribution mirror network for OpenMandriva. Downloads are redirected automatically to the nearest healthy mirror, with ABF as fallback. The tools below let you inspect the network.

Per-mirror age of the last successful sync, probed over HTTP.

Automatic scoring of mirrors on availability and content freshness.
