Mirrors

Distribution mirror network for OpenMandriva. Downloads are redirected automatically to the nearest healthy mirror, with ABF as fallback. Below are the tools to inspect the network.

Mirror statistics
Live Mirrorbits view: world map, per-mirror download volume and sync offset.
Mirror list (example)
Which mirrors serve a given file, with your detected location, distances and weights.
Reliability ranking
Auto-generated table scoring mirrors on availability and content freshness.
Freshness monitor (mirmon)
Per-mirror age of the last successful sync, probed over HTTP.
Set up a mirror
How to become an OpenMandriva mirror: origins, rsync, disk requirements.
OpenMandriva wiki
Project documentation, teams and infrastructure.
